    How to Play

    1. Roles

    Everyone is a Civilian except for the Imposter(s). Civilians know the secret word. Imposters DO NOT know the word, but they blend in!

    2. Interrogation

    Go around in a circle. Each person says exactly one word as a clue to prove they know the secret word, without making it too obvious for the Imposter.

    3. The Imposter's Job

    If you're the Imposter, listen closely to others' clues, guess the topic, and try to say a word that blends in perfectly so you aren't caught.

    4. Voting

    After everyone gives their clue, discuss and vote on who you think the Imposter is. The player with the most votes is eliminated!

    Civilians win if the Imposters are eliminated. Imposters win if they survive!
